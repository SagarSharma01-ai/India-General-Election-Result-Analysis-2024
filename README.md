## ðŸ’¡ Key Findings

### 1. The Coalition Era Returns
- **NDA Alliance:** Secured **292 Seats (54%)**, forming the government.
- **I.N.D.I.A. Alliance:** Secured **234 Seats (43%)**, forming a formidable opposition.
- The gap between the two major blocks has narrowed significantly compared to 2019.

### 2. Party Performance
- **BJP:** 240 Seats (Single Largest Party).
- **INC:** 99 Seats (Major Resurgence).
- **Samajwadi Party (SP):** 37 Seats (Key Player in UP).

### 3. The "Swing" States
- **Uttar Pradesh:** A massive shift where the SP-INC alliance out-performed expectations.
- **Andhra Pradesh & Bihar:** Emerged as the "Kingmaker" states with TDP (16) and JD(U) (12) holding the key to the majority.
